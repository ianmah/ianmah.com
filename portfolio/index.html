    Post Secondary Education

    After graduating from High-School, I plan on attending post-secondary education. My first choice is to go to university, but college is also a possibility. My top two choices for school and their respective programs are:

    UBC

    Faculty of Science. Computer Science, BSc

    After completing this degree I plan on pursing a career as a computer programmer.

    SFU

    School of Computing Science. Software Systems, BSc

    After completing this degree I plan on pursing a career as a computer programmer.

        I feel that a career as a computer programmer would be very good for me, as I am good with computers and technology. Working as a computer programmer requires adaptability and efficiency which are traits I posses. Additionally, I feel as though this job will allow me to use the gifts God has given me. It also provides enough income to raise a family, which is something I would like to do. Typical jobs involve working full time, which is something I would be willing to do, as long as my weekends are free.
